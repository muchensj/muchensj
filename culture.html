Our Journey

Milestones in Our History

A timeline of our achievements and growth over the years.

2014 - Company Founded

Beijing Muchen Shengjing Technology Co., Ltd was established with a vision to transform businesses through innovative technology solutions. Starting with a team of 10 passionate professionals.

2016 - Expansion of Services

Expanded our service portfolio to include mobile application development and cloud solutions. Reached 50 team members and served clients across 10 countries.

2018 - Mobile Management Platform Launch

Launched our flagship mobile management application, receiving widespread acclaim. Achieved 1 million downloads and partnered with major enterprise clients.

2020 - Global Recognition

Recognized as a Top Technology Innovator. Expanded operations to serve clients in 25+ countries with a team of 100+ professionals.

2022 - Strategic Partnerships

Established strategic partnerships with leading global technology providers. Launched enterprise solutions division and achieved ISO certifications.

2024 - Present Day

Serving clients in 30+ countries with 150+ team members. Continuing to innovate and expand our mobile and enterprise solutions portfolio.
